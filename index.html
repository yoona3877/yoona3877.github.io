RESEARCH PROJECT

SUMMER RESEARCH STUDENT

Department of Computer Science @ UofTMay 2018 - Aug 2018

Verbal Autopsy is a method of making prediction of a probable cause of death (CoD) given non-medical data collected by surveyors. This research focuses on making classification of CoD with text narrative using machine learning and NLP. The improvement of Verbal Autopsy will lead to an salient decision of public health plans and funding in countries where no physical autopsy is performed.

RESEARCH STUDENT

Donnelly Centre @ UofTNov 2016 - Apr 2018

Chip-Seq is a technique used to determine interactions between proteins and DNA. Various tools exist for DNA data analysis, but people with no computer science background have difficulty using those tools with diverse functionalities. My research focuses on developing an Chip-Seq data analysis application which produces an DNA data-analyzed table/graph in a simple step.
